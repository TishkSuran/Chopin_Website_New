COMPOSER

Frédéric Chopin

Frédéric Chopin (1810–1849) was a luminary of the Romantic era, known for his expressive piano compositions that continue to captivate audiences worldwide. Discover his timeless music and explore the depth of Romantic-era artistry.

Discover Chopin's Influence

Chopin revolutionised piano technique with his poetic etudes, nocturnes, preludes, and polonaises. His legacy shaped the future of Romantic music, inspiring composers like Liszt, Brahms, and beyond.
